Country Director, Bangladesh at Land O' LakesVenture37 | Program Management, Agribusiness, Market System & Value Chain Development Expert!

Md. Maksudur Rahman is an Agricultural Engineer, topped up with an MBA and MDS. He has worked in various leadership and technical positions with the donor, international organizations, donor funded projects and private sector for more than 24 years. His works focused on technology innovations & its commercialization, value chain development, sustainable livelihoods development and improving food & nutritional security of the poor. His key focus areas lie in maintaining donor relations, promoting pro-poor growth, mainstreaming women economic empowerment, identifying sector and cross sector growth opportunities and incentivizing private sector companies to engage in development in a commercially sustainable manner.

Maksud facilitated MSMEs to improve production technology, product development, collective marketing, contract growing, post-harvest practices in collaboration with the multinational/national companies and lead firms following market for poor (m4p) approaches.

Maksud is familiar with participatory implementation process, monitoring evaluation and learning using different organizational development tools. He possesses demonstrative skills in preparing analytical reports, budgets and case studies. Maksud has experience in regulatory reforms, policy advocacy and investment climate to unlock potential sector growth and generate local and foreign investments through addressing systemic constraints impending economic growth. He has experience in using effective market research tools and techniques; and carried out numerous qualitative and quantitative research on different issues of sectoral development. Maksud is a team player with ability to lead and manage team in matrix structure and able to deal with inter and intra-group conflict.
